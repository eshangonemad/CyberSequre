Guide

1. Be cautious of unexpected contact and verify sources.

2. Don't share personal info unless you’re sure it's necessary.

3. Avoid clicking suspicious links or downloading unknown files.

4. Use strong, unique passwords for each account.

5. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.

6. Trust your instincts—if it feels off, investigate first.

7. Watch for pressure tactics like urgent payment demands.

8. Regularly monitor your accounts for unusual activity.

9. Install and update security software on all devices.

10. Stay informed about common scams and educate others.
